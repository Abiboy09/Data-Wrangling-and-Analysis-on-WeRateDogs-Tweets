We Rate Dogs Data.

This report communicates the insights and display the visualization produced from the wrangled data.

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog.

Insights, Analysis and visualization

Insight 1¶

Most Popular Dog Type:

My analysis report shows that most popular dog type (breed) is Golden retriever with a Labrador retriever taken up the second slot. French Bulldog and Miniature Pinscher appears to be the least popular dog breed based on the data gathered and analysis computed.

Insight 2¶

Find and visualise the most popular Dog Names:

Using the name value counts and ploting bar char for visualisation, our reports shows that the most popular dogs are: Charlie, Cooper, and Lucy.

Insight 3¶

Find the correlation between Retweet and Favorite Tweet Counts:

My analysis and visualization reveals that is a positive correlation between favorite like tweet counts, and retweet count. This makes sense because if you liked a tweet, there is a 65% chance that yo will retweet that particular tweet.
